Phone Stand


Design


I wanted to design a phone stand, but it was rather hard as when looking for inspirationm every and any design for a phone stand was out there already and it was hard to do something new. I knew I wasnted to do something that was a little unqiue. I decided to create a phone stand that can be help in 2 different types of angles that the phone can stand on. I wanted to do the phone stand to be a whole unit, but I realised that it was going to take a very long time to print so i decided to split it up and use a bar to connect it together. It was quite hard to predict if the phonestand can actually withstand the weight of my phone, and so i tried to make the model not as hollow, so that it will not be prone to toppling. I also had to reprint the bar as I did not account for the dimensions error.
