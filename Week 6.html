Week 6

Chess Piece

Firstly, I found a picture of a chess piece on the internet and imported it into Inkscape where I was able to use the 'Bitmap Trace' function to get the outline and imported it back into fusion360. One thing I struggled with was getting the measurements right for the trace. Since it was an online photo I found on the iternet, it was not the ideal size that fitted in the criteria, so I had to play around with the 'Scale' function so that i could get the closest possible dimension and edit it to fit perfectly for my model. To create the base of the chess piece, I used the 'Revolve' feature to help me create a curved surface, to mimic a chess piece. I then used the 'Infill' function so that the model would be more hollow, this is good for pieces that do not require that much weight to it.
