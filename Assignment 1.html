Accessories Box

For the design, I incoporated a drawer as well as a lid, with a design on top, for the storage for jewellry. Some problems I faced when making laser cutting was having the right amount of allowance.


For example, the drawer did not have enough allowance for it to be able to slide in and out easily, which resulted in me having to edit the dimensions multiple times. I also did not use parametrics in some of the dimensions which would have saved me a lot more time when changing the dimensions, and often resuling in warning pop-ups due to the dimensions of some matching and some not. I also had problems with the thickness of the wood.


Since I did not factor in the thickness of the wood, it greatly affected the quality of the product as some joints was not able to be as tight fitting as what I had in mind.
